Healthcare Software Modernization and Platform Adoption

Advisory Support for CT Service Applications

At GE HealthCare, I advised engineering teams, software architects, and product stakeholders on modernizing CT service applications and transitioning from independently developed solutions toward a reusable platform-based architecture. I recommended the adoption of shared Angular libraries, configurable components, plugin-based extension patterns, common services, controlled version management, reusable automation, and framework governance practices.

I supported teams during architecture evaluation, framework onboarding, component design, library integration, testing, and application migration. The recommended platform approach was adopted across more than 15 CT service applications and has remained in active use for more than three years. This improved architectural consistency, reduced duplicated implementation, simplified maintenance, and provided a scalable foundation for additional CT service applications.

  • CT application modernization strategy
  • Reusable Angular library and component adoption
  • Plugin-based architecture recommendations
  • Shared services and version-management guidance
  • Selenium automation and testing strategy
  • Mock-service strategy for independent UI development
  • Framework governance and adoption support
